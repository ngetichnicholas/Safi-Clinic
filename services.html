OUR MEDICAL SERVICES

Name Description
Allergy (Asthma) Care for those with allergies, asthma and immunodeficiency. We strive to control your symptoms and improve your quality of life.
Anesthesiology Administration of anesthetic related to surgery. Staff stays throughout your procedure and closely monitors your pain, anxiety and vital organ function.
Anticoagulation Follow-up care for patients with heart disease, which includes the administration of an anticoagulant drug to help prevent blood clots.
Assisted Living Housing options for seniors and disabled persons. Daily life skill services vary at different locations based on the individual's needs.
Audiology (Hearing) Care for people of all ages with hearing loss, dizziness and balance disorders.
Bariatric Surgery (Weight Loss Surgery) Surgical services including the removal or alteration of a portion of the stomach or digestive tract which can address obesity in adults.
Behavioral Health Providing care and making a positive difference in the lives of those suffering from mental illness, including dependency and relationship issues.
Blood (Hematology) Care and treatment for those with blood diseases and conditions such as lymphoma, anemia and leukemia.
Bones (Orthopedics) Care for individuals with issues related to muscles, bones and joints from the simplest to the most complex orthopedic conditions.
Breast Cancer Care Evaluation, treatment and follow-up for breast cancer care, including annual mammograms for women over age 40.
Breast Care Evaluation, treatment and follow-up for breast concerns, including annual mammograms for women over age 40.
